About an Atom

An atom is a particle of matter that uniquely defines a chemical element. An atom consists of a central nucleus that is surrounded by one or more negatively charged electrons.The nucleus is positively charged and contains one or more relatively heavy particles known as protons and neutrons.Atoms are the basic building blocks of matter. Anything that takes up space and anything with mass is made up of atoms.
